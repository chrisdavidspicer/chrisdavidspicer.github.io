Rules

Click on the dice you want to keep,

then save your points or re-roll and try your luck!

If all 5 dice score, you get to roll again.

But be careful, if you ever roll dice with no value,

you lose all your points for the round!

It takes 350 points in one round to get on the board.

Game ends when a player reaches 5000.

Scoring

1’s = 100 points each

5’s = 50 points each

Three 2’s = 200 points

… 3’s = 300 points

… 4’s = 400 points

… 5’s = 500 points

… 6’s = 600 points

… 1’s = 1000 points

Straight of 12345 = 1500 points
