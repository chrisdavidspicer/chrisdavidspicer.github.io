Rules

Click on the dice you want to keep, then save your points or try your luck with another roll! If all 5 dice score, you get to roll again. But be careful, if you ever roll dice with no value, you lose all your points for the round! Game ends when a player reaches 5000.

  • 1’s = 100 points each
  • 5’s = 50 points each
  • Three 2’s = 200 points
  • … 3’s = 300 points
  • … 4’s = 400 points
  • … 5’s = 500 points
  • … 6’s = 600 points
  • … 1’s = 1000 points
  • Straight of 12345 = 1500 points
